Process settlements for a range of FX products

The FX business utilises a number of products to sell to their clients. The Settlement Obligation Management component will support these products. The supported product set is listed below:

  • FX:Spot trades
  • FX:Forward trades
  • FX:Swap trades
  • FX:NonDeliverableSpot trades
  • FX:NonDeliverableForward trades
