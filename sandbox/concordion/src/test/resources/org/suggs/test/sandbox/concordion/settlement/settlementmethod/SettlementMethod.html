Process a settlement request for a trade with a number of settlement methods

A number of methods exist for settling obligations to settle. For each differing settlement method, different activities will be required to fully settle the obligation. At a high level settlements need to do a range of the following:

  • Cash Management to populate the Nostros
  • Accounting to the books and records
  • Managing settlement risk
  • Payments/receipts across Nostros
