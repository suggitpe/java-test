Process settlement requests for a range of trade states

In the booking of trades, we expect that the vast majority will be booked correctly first time and that no changes are required to the booking. However, we additionally expect that a number will also be booked incorrectly. In the case where a trade has been booked incorrectly there are two options:

  1. Amend the trade: if the change is not an economic change
  2. Cancel the trade: if the change is an economic (assumption is that the trade is rebooked)
